We have the following dictionary: cheese = {'foo': 42, 'fizz': 'hello', 'spam': 'foo'}

What do the following expressions evaluate to?

  1. cheese['foo']
  2. 10 + cheese['foo']
  3. cheese['fizz'] + cheese['fizz']
  4. cheese
  5. cheese[cheese['spam']]



Answers:
  1. 42
  2. 52
  3. 'hellohello'
  4. {'foo': 42, 'fizz': 'hello', 'spam': 'foo'}
  5. 42
